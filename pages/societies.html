Thapar Model United Nations (TUMUN), an 8 year old conference, is one of the most prestigious conferences of North India. It is a 2-day conference and is organized in 2 levels: Intra and Inter.
The aim of Intra MUN is to introduce the first-year TIET students to the culture of the MUN whereas Inter MUN involves the participation of more than 350 delegates from renowned colleges of North India.
The society has been successful in sending a delegation to Harvard National MUN and also is proud of its winners of the Delhi circuit MUN’s, JMC, DelTech, to name a few.
